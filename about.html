Colegio de Montalban

Experience Excellence

Institution's History

2003: Founded as Pamantasan ng Montalban via Ordinance No. 03-24.

2004: Began operations to provide vocational and higher education.

2014: Officially renamed to Colegio de Montalban (Ordinance No. 14-15)

Present: Operating under RA 10931 (Universal Access to Quality Tertiay Education Act), providing free tuition.

Vision

Transform individuals into responsible citizens capable of
meeting the challenges of modern times.

Mission

To prepare students o become useful citizens, God-fearing,
value-oriented, and disciplined individuals who are universally competitive
professionals engaged in uplifting the quality of life in the community.

Objectives

Colegio de Montalban aims to produce graduates who show
high standards of learning and demonstrates strong
performance in their chose field

Goals

Achieve high productivity, global competetiveness,
and social responsibility among professionals.
