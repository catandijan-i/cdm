Welcome to Colegio de Montalban!

Colegio de Montalban, formerly known as Pamantasan ng Montalban is a government-funded college in Rodriguez, Rizal.
Cdm offers programs from business and entrepreneurship, computer sciences, to education. Cdm aspires to prepare
students to be ready for the future by the help of our dedicated faculty and administrators.

Vision

Transform individuals into responsible citizens capable of
meeting the challenges of modern times.

Mission

To prepare students to become useful citizens, God-fearing,
value-oriented, and disciplined individuals who are universally competitive
professionals engaged in uplifting the quality of life in the community.

Objectives

Colegio de Montalban aims to produce graduates who show
high standards of learning and demonstrate strong
performance in their chosen field.

Goals

Achieve high productivity, global competitiveness,
and social responsibility among professionals.
